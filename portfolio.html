HealthTrac Weight Management Platform

Client - SetPoint Health

SetPoint Health, a leading provider of weight management solutions, partnered with our company to enhance their HealthTrac platform. The goal was to improve user experience, streamline third-party integrations, and ensure compliance with the latest security protocols.

Case Study: Enhancing HealthTrac for Optimal User Experience

Challenge: SetPoint Health's HealthTrac platform required software maintenance, modernization, and enhanced functionality to cater to the evolving needs of over 200 practices. Key challenges included:

  • Outdated Technologies: The existing software stack needed upgrading to leverage the benefits of the latest technologies.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Seamless integration with popular health and fitness devices, such as IHealth, Garmin, Withings, GoogleFit, Fitbit, Dexcom (CGM), Biosense, Twilio, Seca, and Nutritionix, was necessary to provide comprehensive user data.
  • 3. Security and Compliance: Upgrading the platform to support TLS 1.2 Protocol was essential to enhance security and meet industry standards.

Solution: Our team executed a comprehensive strategy to address the challenges and optimize the HealthTrac platform:

  • Technology Upgrade: We modernized the software stack, leveraging the latest technologies, frameworks, and libraries to improve performance, scalability, and maintainability.
  • Seamless Integrations: We successfully integrated a wide range of third-party services and devices, enabling users to synchronize their data effortlessly.
  • Security Enhancement: We diligently upgraded the platform to support TLS 1.2 Protocol, ensuring secure data transmission and compliance with industry standards.
  • Intuitive Dashboard: We designed a visually appealing and user-friendly dashboard, empowering practice managers to access vital user data through interactive tiles.

Result: The enhancements made to the HealthTrac platform yielded significant results:

  • Improved User Experience: The upgraded platform offered enhanced performance, intuitive navigation, and synchronized data from various devices, resulting in a seamless user experience.
  • Streamlined Operations: Practice managers could efficiently oversee user data through the intuitive dashboard, improving workflow and decision-making.
  • Enhanced Security: The TLS 1.2 Protocol implementation bolstered data security, ensuring safe transmission of sensitive health information.
  • Expanded Business Reach: The successful integration with popular health and fitness devices attracted new practices, expanding SetPoint Health's customer base.

ITC Systems Cash Card Management Systems

Client - ITC Systems

ITC Systems, a leading international provider of cashless Integrated Transaction Control Systems, partnered with our company to enhance their software solutions. Our goal was to improve the user experience, streamline payment integrations, and provide seamless access control and printing solutions.

Case Study: Modernizing Cash Card Management Systems

Challenge: ITC Systems' cash card management systems required modernization to cater to the needs of various institutions and environments. Key challenges included:

  • User Experience: The need for an easy-to-use, secure, and customizable web-based application that integrates seamlessly with back-end databases.
  • Payment Integrations: Integration with popular payment gateway services, including Authorize.net, Govolution, First Data, Elavon, and others.
  • Single Sign-On (SSO): Implementation of Shibboleth-based SSO for enhanced security and user convenience.
  • Printing Solutions: Developing a cloud-based printing solution that enables users to submit and retrieve print jobs securely and easily.

Solution: Our team executed a comprehensive strategy to address the challenges and optimize ITC Systems' software solutions:

  • Software Modernization: We modernized the software solutions, leveraging the latest technologies and frameworks to improve performance, scalability, and user experience.
  • Seamless Payment Integrations: We successfully integrated popular payment gateway services, enabling secure and efficient transactions for users.
  • Shibboleth-based SSO: We implemented Shibboleth-based single sign-on to enhance security and simplify user authentication across various systems.
  • Cloud-based Printing Solution: We developed a robust printing solution, GoPrint, enabling users to securely submit and retrieve print jobs from any enabled location using usernames or emails and optional passwords.

Result: The modernization efforts and software enhancements resulted in significant improvements:

  • Enhanced User Experience: The intuitive web-based application and the AVROportal module empowered users to manage their accounts, view transactions, and access various services conveniently.
  • Streamlined Payment Processing: The seamless integration with payment gateway services simplified and secured transactions for users and vendors, ensuring reliable revenue collection.
  • Improved Security: The implementation of Shibboleth-based SSO and adherence to modern security protocols enhanced user authentication and data privacy.
  • Efficient Printing Solution: The GoPrint software provided a user-friendly and secure platform for submitting and retrieving print jobs, enhancing productivity and convenience.

Albiware Customizable Business Management Software

Client - Albiware

Albiware, a leading provider of customizable business management software, partnered with our company to develop and enhance their software solution. The goal was to provide businesses with a flexible platform to track key performance indicators (KPIs), streamline workflows, generate custom reports, and integrate with essential tools.

Case Study: Empowering Businesses with Customizable Software

Challenge: Albiware recognized the need for a versatile software solution that could adapt to the unique requirements of different businesses. Key challenges included:

  • Customization: Businesses needed the ability to choose which KPIs to track and set up workflows according to their specific needs.
  • Insightful Reporting: Generating custom reports was essential to gain powerful insights and analysis for efficient job management and accounting.
  • Flexibility in Workflows: Businesses required the flexibility to tailor certain workflows to align with their unique business processes.
  • Accessibility and Integration: Workforce mobility demanded the availability of Albi software on desktop and mobile devices, along with seamless integration with popular tools such as Zapier, QuickBooks, Encircle, Company Cam, and Kahi.

Solution: Our team developed a comprehensive strategy to address the challenges and empower businesses with a customizable software solution:

  • Custom Reports: We designed a reporting module that allowed businesses to generate custom reports, providing them with valuable insights and analysis across various aspects of their operations.
  • Tailored Workflows: Albiware's software was designed to accommodate the unique workflow requirements of different businesses, ensuring optimal efficiency and productivity.
  • Mobile and Desktop Accessibility: Albiware provided both Albi desktop and Albi mobile applications, enabling users to access the software and perform tasks anytime, anywhere, whether in the office or out in the field.
  • Seamless Integrations: We implemented seamless integrations with popular tools such as Zapier, QuickBooks, Encircle, Company Cam, and Kahi, allowing businesses to bring all their data into one centralized platform for enhanced efficiency and convenience.

Result: The implementation of Albiware's customizable software solution resulted in significant benefits for businesses:

  • Enhanced Efficiency: Businesses were able to track their chosen KPIs and streamline workflows according to their specific needs, resulting in improved operational efficiency.
  • Powerful Insights: The custom reports provided businesses with valuable insights and analysis, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved job management and accounting.
  • Workforce Mobility: The availability of Albi desktop and Albi mobile applications allowed businesses to work seamlessly regardless of their location, increasing productivity and responsiveness.
  • Centralized Data: The integrations with popular tools facilitated the consolidation of data into one platform, reducing manual tasks and improving data accuracy.
